## 🔗 Connecting Your Social Media

### **Facebook Connection** 📘

#### **How to Connect Facebook**

1. **Go to Your Settings**

   - Click your profile picture in the top right corner
   - Select "Profile Settings"
   - Click on the "Integrations" tab

2. **Link Your Facebook Account**

   - Click the "Connect Facebook" button
   - You'll be taken to Facebook to approve the connection
   - Facebook will ask for your permission to let SaMMy:
     - Post to your Facebook Pages
     - Read your Page statistics
     - See your list of Pages
     - Access your basic profile information

3. **Choose Your Page**
   - Select which Facebook Page you want to manage
   - You must be an administrator of the Page
   - Your connection stays active for 60 days, then you'll need to reconnect

#### **What You Can Do with Facebook**

- Post text updates to your Page
- Share images and photos
- Schedule posts for later
- Track how your posts are performing
- **Post Types:** Text, images, links, videos
- **Analytics:** Post engagement, reach, impressions

### **Twitter Connection** 🐦

#### **How to Connect Twitter**

1. **Go to Your Settings**

   - Click your profile picture and select "Profile Settings"
   - Go to the "Integrations" tab

2. **Link Your Twitter Account**

   - Click the "Connect Twitter" button
   - You'll be taken to Twitter to approve the connection
   - Twitter will ask for permission to let SaMMy:
     - Read your tweets and profile
     - Post tweets on your behalf
     - Upload images and videos

3. **What You Can Do with Twitter**
   - Post tweets (up to 280 characters)
   - Share images, GIFs, and videos
   - Create tweet threads (multiple connected tweets)
   - Schedule tweets for later

### **Slack Connection** 💬

#### **How to Connect Slack**

1. **Connect Your Workspace**

   - Click "Connect Slack" in your settings
   - Choose which Slack workspace to connect

2. **Choose Your Channels**

   - Select which channels SaMMy can post to
   - You can update this anytime

3. **What You Can Do with Slack**
   - Post messages to team channels
   - Share updates with your team
   - Schedule messages for specific times
   - Share files and images

## 🤖 Creating Content with AI

### **How the AI Helps You**

SaMMy uses artificial intelligence to help you write social media posts. Think of it like having a smart assistant who:

- Learns your writing style
- Suggests content based on what you want to say
- Adapts posts for different social media platforms
- Saves you time and effort

### **Creating Your First Post**

#### **Step 1: Tell the AI What You Want**

Simply type what kind of post you want to create. For example:

```
💡 Try These Ideas:
• "Write a motivational Monday post about staying productive"
• "Create a tip about online security"
• "Make a fun post about coffee and Mondays"
• "Announce our new product launching next week"
• "Share an inspiring quote about teamwork"
```

#### **Step 2: Wait for the AI (Takes 5-15 Seconds)**

The AI is:

- Looking at your previous posts to match your style
- Creating content that sounds like you
- Making sure it fits on each social media platform

#### **Step 3: Review and Edit**

- The AI will show you several options
- You can edit any of the suggestions
- See a preview of how it will look when posted
- The system automatically counts characters for each platform

### **Tips for Better Results**

- **Be Specific:** Instead of "write a post," try "write an exciting post about our weekend sale"
- **Mention Your Audience:** "for small business owners" or "for fitness enthusiasts"
- **Set the Tone:** "professional," "casual," "funny," or "inspirational"
- **Include Key Points:** Mention important details you want to include

## 📅 Scheduling Your Posts

### **Post Right Away**

1. Create or generate your content
2. Choose which platforms to post on (Facebook, Twitter, Slack)
3. Click "Post Now"
4. Your post goes live immediately!

### **Schedule for Later**

1. Create your content
2. Click "Schedule Post"
3. Pick a date and time:
   - Click on the calendar to choose a date
   - Select the time you want it to post
   - Don't worry about time zones - we handle that for you!
4. Choose which platforms to post on
5. Click "Schedule" to confirm

### **Managing Your Scheduled Posts**

- **View All Posts:** See everything you've scheduled in one place
- **Edit Posts:** Change the content, time, or which platforms to post on

- **Automatic Posting:** Your posts will go live at the scheduled time automatically

## 📜 Managing Your Conversation History

### **What is Conversation History?**

Every time you chat with SaMMy's AI, we save the conversation so you can come back to it later. This is helpful when you want to:

- Continue a previous conversation
- See what posts you created before
- Keep track of your content ideas
- Pick up where you left off

### **How to Access Your History**

1. Click the **"History"** button (looks like a clock icon)
2. You'll see a list of all your past conversations
3. Each conversation shows:
   - A title (based on what you were talking about)
   - A preview of your last message
   - How many messages you exchanged
   - Which platform it was for (Facebook, Twitter, etc.)
   - When you last used it

### **What You Can Do**

- **Load a Conversation:** Click "Load" to continue where you left off
- **Delete a Conversation:** Click the trash icon to remove conversations you don't need anymore
- **Browse Your History:** Scroll through to find old ideas and posts

### **Benefits**

- **Never Lose Your Work:** All your conversations are saved
- **Easy Reference:** Find old posts and ideas quickly
- **Continue Conversations:** No need to re-explain everything to the AI
- **Stay Organized:** Keep all your content planning in one place

## 🛠️ Common Questions & Solutions

### **Facebook Connection Issues**

**Problem:** Can't connect Facebook or connection keeps breaking

**Try These Solutions:**

1. ✅ Make sure you're an administrator of the Facebook Page (not just a member)
2. ✅ Check that your Facebook Page is published (not in draft mode)
3. ✅ Try disconnecting and connecting again
4. ✅ Clear your browser's cache and cookies
5. ✅ Try using a different web browser

**Still Not Working?**

- Make sure the Facebook Page exists and is active
- Verify you're logged into the correct Facebook account
- Check if Facebook is having any service issues

```
1. Check Facebook App Status:
   - App ID: 839763468733190
   - Verify app is "Live" not "Development"

**Still Not Working?**
- Make sure the Facebook Page exists and is active
- Verify you're logged into the correct Facebook account
- Check if Facebook is having any service issues

### **Twitter Connection Issues**

**Problem:** Can't connect Twitter or posts aren't going through

**Try These Solutions:**
1. ✅ Make sure your Twitter account is in good standing (not suspended)
2. ✅ Verify you're logged into the correct Twitter account
3. ✅ Check that your tweets fit within 280 characters
4. ✅ Try disconnecting and reconnecting your Twitter account

### **AI Content Problems**

**Problem:** AI is taking too long or not working

**What's Happening:**
- **Taking 5-15 Seconds:** This is normal! The AI is thinking and creating content for you
- **Not Generating Content:** There might be a temporary issue with the AI service
- **Content Doesn't Sound Like You:** The AI learns from your posts - the more you use it, the better it gets!

**Get Better Results:**
- Be more specific in your request (instead of "write a post," try "write an exciting post about our weekend sale")
- Tell the AI about your audience ("for small businesses" or "for pet owners")
- Mention the tone you want ("professional," "funny," "inspiring")

### **Scheduled Posts Not Publishing**

**Problem:** Posts aren't going live at the scheduled time

**Check These Things:**
1. ✅ Make sure your social media accounts are still connected
2. ✅ Verify the scheduled time hasn't passed
3. ✅ Check that you chose the correct date and time
4. ✅ Ensure the platform (Facebook/Twitter) isn't having issues

### **Login Problems**

**Problem:** Can't log in or keep getting logged out

**Solutions:**
- **Forgot Password:** Click "Forgot Password" and check your email
- **Browser Issues:** Try clearing your browser's cache and cookies (Google: "how to clear cache in [your browser name]")
- **Logged Out After 7 Days:** This is normal for security - just log back in
- **Wrong Email:** Double-check you're using the right email address

### **Payment Problems**

**Problem:** Can't process payment or subscription issues

**Try These:**
1. ✅ Check that your credit card information is correct
2. ✅ Make sure your card hasn't expired
3. ✅ Verify your card has enough funds available
4. ✅ Try a different credit card
5. ✅ Contact your bank if they're blocking the charge

### **Slow Performance**

**Problem:** App is running slowly

**Quick Fixes:**
- **Refresh the page:** Press F5 or the refresh button
- **Check your internet:** Make sure you have a good connection
- **Close other tabs:** Too many open tabs can slow things down
- **Try another browser:** Sometimes switching browsers helps
- **Restart your browser:** Close it completely and open it again
```
